Internet

Our college has dedicated Internet Leased Lines  
of 14 Mbps from Airtel. Internet connection is 
provided to the students in the college in 
various places like the library, labs and 
Department Head offices. Apart from this, 
Internet is also provided to the hostel students 
in a separate 'Net Room’ that facilitates their 
easy access to the internet, thereby enabling 
them to stay updated with the latest advances in 
the field. Online placement tests for all the 
students are conducted every week.
